About This Report

The Industrial Training Report titled "Solar Tech Training Report" stands as an accurate record of the endeavors undertaken by our team, conducted under the guidance of Sanjay Mehta, the esteemed trainer at NSIC. It is a culmination of the knowledge and skills acquired during the intensive 2-week program offered by the National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) from June 12th to June 22nd, 2023.

The significance of this report lies in its alignment with the syllabus for Industrial Training, a mandatory component for the conferral of the B. Tech. degree in Electrical Engineering from Aliah University.
